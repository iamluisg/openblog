## Overview

Everyday English is a comprehensive reference application that helps Korean speakers learn and use English in everyday situations. Unlike traditional language learning apps, this focuses on practical, real-world scenarios.

## Key Features

- **Scenario-based learning** covering common daily situations
- **Phrase categories** including shopping, dining, transportation, and more
- **Native speaker audio** for accurate pronunciation
- **Offline access** to all content
- **Search functionality** to quickly find needed phrases

## Use Cases

Perfect for:

- Travelers visiting English-speaking countries
- Students studying abroad
- Anyone learning conversational English
- Quick reference in real-time situations

## Content Organization

The app organizes phrases by:

- Daily activities (eating, shopping, etc.)
- Social situations (greetings, small talk)
- Emergency situations
- Common questions and responses

## Design Philosophy

The app was designed with simplicity and speed in mind. Users can quickly find the phrase they need without navigating through complex menus or lessons.

## User Feedback

Users particularly appreciate the app's focus on practical phrases that are actually used in everyday conversation, rather than formal textbook English.
